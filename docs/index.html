1. Client Onboarding

Lock the working setup before content production starts.

Week 0 / Start
  • Confirm main client contact and approval person. Decide who gives final sign-off for captions, visuals, posting dates, and profile changes.
  • Confirm approved retainer scope. Facebook, Instagram, XiaoHongShu, TikTok, content planning, captions, creative direction, asset selection, and reporting.
  • Confirm Month 1 objective. Foundation, profile clarity, trust-building, consistency, and baseline measurement.
  • Set weekly approval rhythm. Planning, asset collection, content batching, client approval, publishing, and weekly performance checks.

2. Access And Channel Setup

Make sure all active channels are usable before the first content batch.

Setup
  • Get Facebook page access. Request admin, editor, or Meta Business Suite access.
  • Get Instagram access for @hgservicesmy. Confirm login, Meta link, profile edit access, and posting access.
  • Confirm XiaoHongShu and TikTok account status. If active, get access. If not active, prepare setup and profile positioning for each channel.
  • Check website link. Use the existing website as a trust and reference point where suitable.
  • Confirm WhatsApp/contact path. Make the preferred enquiry route clear from active social channels.
  • Confirm access to basic insights. Needed for reach, engagement, profile visits, WhatsApp clicks, DMs, and enquiry tracking.

3. Positioning And Message Alignment

Keep the message sharp: HG should be known for reliable reinstatement and restoration support.

Strategy
  • Lock the core positioning statement. Reliable contractor support team for office and residential reinstatement, restoration, handover, and site support works.
  • Confirm priority Month 1 services. Office reinstatement, residential restoration, move-out handover, make-good works, rorobin, lorry, scaffold, debris handling, and site coordination.
  • Confirm target audiences. Office tenants, building managers, property managers, homeowners, landlords, agents, and tenants preparing for handover.

4. Channel Audit And Profile Optimization

Make every channel easier to understand and easier to enquire from.

Foundation
  • Audit Facebook. Review service description, visuals, recent content, contact details, and enquiry path.
  • Audit Instagram. Review bio, name field, highlights, pinned posts, contact button, and visual consistency.
  • Audit XiaoHongShu and TikTok. Review account setup, profile description, visuals, short-form content readiness, and search-friendly service wording.
  • Update bio language. Emphasize office and residential reinstatement, restoration, handover, and contractor support.
  • Create or refine Instagram highlights. Before/After, Office, Residential, Process, Safety, Rorobin, Scaffold, and Reviews.
  • Check contact visibility. WhatsApp or preferred contact route should be obvious from every active channel.

5. Asset Collection And Sorting

Turn real project work into a usable content library.

20-30 assets
  • Request 20 to 30 usable real project assets from HG. Prioritize clear photos and short clips that show real work, process, tools, teams, and results.
  • Sort assets by project stage. Before photos, work-in-progress photos, after photos, short site videos, team photos, and equipment photos.
  • Tag usable content angles. Before/after, checklists, project highlights, Stories, XiaoHongShu notes, and case study-style posts.

6. Content Pillars And Monthly Mix

Use the approved split so the month balances education, proof, capability, and authority.

Approved mix
Proof of Work: 20% Before/after posts, completed projects, site progress, and handover results.
Education: 45% Move-out checklists, building rules, floor protection, debris handling, and common mistakes.
Capability: 20% Team on site, lorries, rorobin, scaffold, tools, day/night operations, and coordination.
Authority: 15% Case studies, credentials, testimonials, project experience, and trust-building proof.

9. Production, Approval, And Publishing

Keep production weekly so approvals stay manageable.

Execution
  • Draft captions for Facebook, Instagram, and XiaoHongShu. Facebook can carry slightly more explanation; Instagram should stay visual and practical; XiaoHongShu should be more searchable.
  • Prepare simple visual templates. Before/after posts, checklist posts, project highlights, and case study-style posts.
  • Submit weekly content batch for approval. Include posting date, platform, visual, caption, call to action, and any notes needed from HG.
  • Publish and monitor content. Track comments, DMs, WhatsApp clicks, enquiry quality, saves, shares, and useful client feedback.

10. Month 1 Reporting And Next Step

Close the month with a baseline and clear Month 2 recommendation.

Baseline report
  • Create the Month 1 baseline report. Include content output, reach, engagement, saves, shares, profile visits, WhatsApp clicks, DMs, enquiries, and enquiry quality.
  • Identify early content winners. Look for the strongest pillars, formats, topics, and calls to action based on actual performance.
  • Recommend Month 2 direction. Move toward mini case studies, recurring content series, stronger reels, and light boosting only if organic winners are clear.
